    emblaster is short for poem blaster and EmilyBlaster. It is inspired by and based on EmilyBlaster, a game in Gabrielle Zevin's Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow.

    This game features the poems: That Love is All There Is by Emily Dickinson, Meditation at Lagunitas by Robert Hass, and Howl by Allen Ginsberg.

    This game was created by Justin Wang.
